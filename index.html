ENGINEERED FOR PERFORMANCE

Sri Sai Vinayaka CNC Technics is a premier Hyderabad-based precision engineering company with over 15+ years of expertise in CNC machining and wire cut services.

We are a growth-focused engineering partner for manufacturers who need reliable, high-precision industrial components delivered on time. Our state-of-the-art facility ensures that every component meets the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.

  • High Precision Machining
  • Strict Quality Control
  • On-Time Delivery
  • 100% Quality Assurance

We work with a wide range of materials, executing micro-tolerances with absolute repeatability:

  • Materials Handled: Stainless Steel (SS304, SS316, etc.), Mild Steel, Tool Steels (D2, H13, WPS, High Carbon High Chromium), Aluminum Alloys, Brass, Copper, Titanium, and Exotic Materials.
  • Core Operations: High-precision Wire Cut EDM, CNC Milling & VMC, CNC Turning, EDM Sparking, Surface Grinding, and custom fixture design.
  • Sectors Served: Aerospace, Defense & Military, Automotive, Medical Devices, Tooling, Mold & Die industries, and General Engineering.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing capability ensures micron-level accuracy for every design:

  • Multi-Axis VMC Milling: Delivering intricate component profiles, close tolerances, and high-speed repeatable batch production.
  • Precision Wire EDM: Handling complex geometries, tapered cuts, and hard-alloy processing with positioning accuracy of up to 3 to 5 microns.
  • Calibrated Metrology: Strict adherence to dimension control using Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM), height gauges, and calibrated dial instrumentation.

Over 15 years of industry excellence is backed by our strict professional code:

  • 100% Quality Assurance: Every single batch of products goes through strict metrology checks with full documentation support.
  • Customer-Centric Turnaround: Dedicated production scheduling to match tight lead-times and rapid prototyping demands.
  • Technical Advisory: In-house engineering team to assist in design optimization and raw material selection for best tool efficiency.
